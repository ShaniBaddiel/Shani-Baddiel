What do matches do?

 Matches are people who contribute highly to crowdfunding funds. They are in fact the backbone of the whole operation. Matches are prepared to not only contribute to a campaign, but also to donate more than anyone else has. So if someone would donate a certain sum of money, matches would be willing to double, triple, or even quadruple that sum donated. They are prepared to do this for any amount contributed! This makes the crowdfunding campaign stand a chance of much better success. But here's a catch! The matches are only prepared to pay up if the target sum has been reached a certain number of hours since the launch of the project. Otherwise, if the campaign proved a failure, the matches reclaim their money and the school, company or business goes away, completely empty handed. As we have been able to reach our target in time, our matches have generously tripled each sum of money that has been contributed, enabling us to purchase our much awaited buses! We owe all our donors a big thank you!
